# nai

Multi-provider AI content generation (Claude, OpenAI, Gemini).

## Key Classes
- `AiGeneratorInterface` — provider abstraction
- DI Extension: `AiExtension`

## Conventions
- PHP 8.2+ with `declare(strict_types=1)`
- PSR-4 autoloading in `src/`
- Nette DI Extension for service registration
- PHPStan level 9
- Part of `jansuchanek/*` on Packagist
- GitHub: https://github.com/JanSuchanek/nai
